## [M] CONTINUITY

- no extra characters unless explicitly allowed
- keep single-frame language (no collage/panels)

## [M] FORBIDDEN

- random new props that break canon lock
- visual mismatch to audio state (e.g. ZERO_POINT but flashy visuals)

- visual prompt ref
- lore trigger ref (even short)
