## [M] ENTITY_HEADER
- ENTITY_NAME: MUS_ALBUM_TO_TRACK
- ENTITY_CLASS: ORC
- UID: UE.V2.ENT.ORC.MUS.ALBUM_TO_TRACK.001

## [M] PURPOSE
Делит ALBUM_TOKEN на TRACK_PLAN: слоты треков, роли (intro/banger/calm/finale), вариативность, правила названий, длительности.

## [M] INPUTS / OUTPUTS
- Inputs: [ALBUM_TOKEN, MUS_BRIEF, STYLE_ROUTE_TOKEN]
- Outputs: [TRACK_PLAN, FAIL_CODE?]

## [M] TOKEN_SCHEMA (TRACK_PLAN)
- TRACK_COUNT
- SLOTS: [{index, role, energy, bpm_hint, key_hint?, lyric_theme_hint}]
- VARIANTS_POLICY
- NAMING_RULES

## [M] GATES
- PASS: план выполнимый и согласован
- FAIL: запрос неясен (сколько треков, какой формат)

## [M] SPC PEER ROLES (NON-ENG)
- Works with: [QA, CTL]
- Handoff: TRACK_PLAN -> LYRIC_BRIEF_FACTORY / PROMPT_PACKAGER / METADATA_FACTORY
