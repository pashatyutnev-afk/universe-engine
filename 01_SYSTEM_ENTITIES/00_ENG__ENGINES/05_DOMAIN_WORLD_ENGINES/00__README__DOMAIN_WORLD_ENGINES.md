## 1. PURPOSE

Domain World Engines define **how the world is structured, ruled, and evolves**.

They provide:
- world structure (spaces, factions, maps, layers)
- world laws (physics, magic, social rules)
- timeline & epochs (historical segmentation)
- civilization modeling (culture, institutions)
- conflict & power systems
- geopolitics (actors + moves)
- economy & resources (flows, scarcity)
- technology & magic (capabilities, limits)
- mythology & belief (meaning systems)
- environment & ecology (biomes, cycles)

These engines produce *world artifacts* used by narrative and production layers.
