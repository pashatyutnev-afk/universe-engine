## PURPOSE

Атмосфера = не “описания”.  
Атмосфера = **давление среды**, которое влияет на:
- решения персонажей
- ощущение опасности/безопасности
- читаемость мира (реальность, чуждость, сакральность)

---

## INPUTS

- Location/world context
- Current tension level
- Cultural layer (belief/rituals)
- Weather/time/crowd density (if applicable)

---

## OUTPUTS

- Atmosphere recipe
- Pressure vectors (what pushes characters)
- Environmental signals list (what audience feels)
- Atmosphere-to-action hooks

---

## ATMOSPHERE RECIPE (CANON)

- SETTING ANCHORS (3–6 конкретных опор)
- PRESSURE VECTORS (cold, noise, crowd, surveillance, decay…)
- SIGNALS (sound/light/space behavior)
- THREAT READABILITY (hidden / obvious / ambiguous)
- SAFETY POCKETS (if any)
- ACTION HOOKS (how atmosphere causes actions)

---

## PROCEDURE

1) Pick anchors (immutable facts of place)
2) Add 2–4 pressure vectors
3) Define signals (what’s perceived)
4) Decide threat readability
5) Identify safety pockets (contrast)
6) Tie at least 2 vectors to character behavior

---

## VALIDATION RULES

- AT1: Atmosphere must influence choices, not just look cool.
- AT2: Signals must be consistent (lighting/noise rules).
- AT3: Pressure vectors must fit world law.
- AT4: Contrast (pockets) increases clarity; use deliberately.
