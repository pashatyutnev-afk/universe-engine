## PURPOSE

Добавляет “случайность” как:
- шум системы
- неполную информацию
- человеческий фактор
- погодные/технические сбои
НО: без разрушения причинности.

---

## INPUTS

- Current event schedule
- System vulnerabilities
- Risk tolerance (low/med/high)
- Desired uncertainty zones

---

## OUTPUTS

- Randomness injection plan
- Chaos events list (small/medium)
- Fairness constraints (anti-deus-ex-machina)
- Post-randomness reconciliation notes

---

## CHAOS TOOLKIT

- MISINFORMATION (ошибка данных)
- ACCIDENT (поломка/травма)
- WEATHER SHIFT (окно закрыто)
- HUMAN ERROR (просрочка/паника)
- LUCK (редко и с ценой)
- OPPORTUNITY (случайная находка)

---

## PROCEDURE

1) Define uncertainty zones (where randomness allowed)
2) Define max impact per zone
3) Select injection type (toolkit)
4) Ensure:
   - cause exists (even if mundane)
   - effect bounded
   - mitigation exists
5) Apply to schedule as perturbations

---

## VALIDATION RULES

- RC1: Randomness cannot solve core conflict for free.
- RC2: Randomness must be explainable post-factum (mundane cause).
- RC3: Impacts are bounded by risk tolerance.
- RC4: Mitigation/counter must exist.
